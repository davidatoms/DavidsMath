## Technical Details

**Format**: GIF (Graphics Interchange Format)  
**Frame Rate**: 1-2 FPS (slow enough to observe)  
**Size**: 70-284 KB (small enough to email/post)  
**Resolution**: 800x800 to 1200x900 (high quality)  
**Frames**: 11 frames (0-10 iterations)  
**Loop**: Infinite (keeps playing)  

**Software**: Julia + Plots.jl  
**Generation time**: ~30 seconds total  
**Reproducible**: Run `julia create_animation.jl`
